Map Design Tip
Pick a walkable, urban neighborhood or downtown. The best maps cover 2–4 square miles with clear boundaries like rivers, highways, or rail lines.
Tap + New Zone to start drawing. Tap a completed zone to edit its vertices.
Aim for 12–20 zones. Keep them roughly equal in size (0.1–0.5 sq mi). Use natural boundaries like streets or rivers.
Tap + Place Landmark then tap the map, or discover real points of interest in your zones.
Place 1–2 landmarks per zone. Choose recognizable places: statues, murals, historic buildings, unique storefronts.
Draw transit lines manually or import real-world routes from OpenStreetMap.
